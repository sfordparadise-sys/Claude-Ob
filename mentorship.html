How It Works

Learn It from
Someone Who's
Lived It.

BuilderPilot mentorship isn't a course or a webinar. It's a direct conversation with Stephen Ford — 20+ years running low-rise residential production sites across Ontario.

The things that take most supers years to figure out on their own don't have to. The morning walk, the trade conversation, the deficiency process, how to protect yourself on a Tarion file — this is the kind of guidance that comes from actually doing it.

Contact to learn more about availability and what a mentorship engagement looks like.

Site Supervision Fundamentals

The daily rhythm, trade management, QC walks, scheduling decisions, and how to build a site that runs itself when you're not looking.

Ontario Regulatory Navigation

OBC, Tarion, ON1Call locates, the PDO process — understanding what the paperwork means and how to protect yourself and the homeowner.

Career Development

Moving from trades to supervision, building a track record, developing leadership instincts, and knowing when to escalate vs. when to own it.

Production Builder Context

Running volume. Managing multiple units, tight schedules, builder-side reporting, and how to keep pace without losing quality.

Who It's For

If You're On

Mentorship is available for people who are serious about building a real career in residential construction supervision.
